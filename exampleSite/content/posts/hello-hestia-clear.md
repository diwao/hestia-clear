Hestia Clear is designed for quiet, readable publishing. This sample post keeps the structure simple so the home page, post title, date, and body text can be checked quickly.

The layout favors generous spacing, clear typography, and a minimal article list. It works well for personal notes, engineering logs, and small editorial sites.

## A Simple Writing Flow

Start with a concise idea, add enough context for the reader, and keep the page free from unnecessary decoration. The theme should stay out of the way while the content does the work.

You can use links like [the Hugo documentation](https://gohugo.io/documentation/) inside normal paragraphs. The link treatment is intentionally direct and readable.

## What to Check

- The article appears on the home page.
- The date uses the theme's default format.
- The post body has comfortable line length and spacing.
- The footer author profile appears after the article navigation.

> Minimal themes still need complete states. This post helps verify the default reading path.
